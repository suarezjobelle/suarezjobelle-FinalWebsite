ABOUT US

Webtoons are a type of digital comic that originated in South Korea. While webtoons were mostly unknown outside of the country during their inception, there has been a surge in popularity internationally thanks in part to most comics being read on smartphones and computers now due to the popularity increase. This Website is all about the different genre and stories in webtoon. It shows all the recommended stories of the author of the website and most popular stories of all time. All of the content used are not from the author and it's only for educational purposes. I hope you enjoyed your visit in our simple website about webtoon comics!
